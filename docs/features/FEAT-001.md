# Morning Brief

**ID:** FEAT-001 | **Module:** MOD-001 | **Status:** draft | **Priority:** high

Daily priorities briefing covering labor, applications, and risks delivered in ≤5 minutes.

**Owner:** Product Manager

## PRD Summary

### Problem
Superintendents need a quick way to understand daily priorities across labor, applications, and risks without manually reviewing multiple systems or reports.

### Goals
1. Provide comprehensive daily overview in ≤5 minutes
2. Surface critical labor assignments and availability
3. Highlight application windows and weather triggers
4. Flag risks and potential issues requiring attention

### Success Metrics
- **Time to review daily priorities:** ≤5 minutes
- **Daily engagement rate:** 90%
- **Issues caught proactively:** 85%

### Scope
**In Scope:**
- Labor priorities and crew assignments
- Application windows and weather-triggered tasks
- Risk alerts and potential conflicts
- Weather summary and forecast impact
- Resource availability status

**Out of Scope:**
- Deep analytics and historical trends
- Detailed financial reports
- Long-term planning views

### Dependencies
- Weather integration must be available
- Labor scheduling system must be implemented
- Application triggers engine must be operational
