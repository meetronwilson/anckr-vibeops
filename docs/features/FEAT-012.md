# Compliance Ledger

**ID:** FEAT-012 | **Module:** MOD-002 | **Status:** draft | **Priority:** critical

Complete audit trail of who applied what products, when, and where for regulatory compliance.

**Owner:** Product Manager

## PRD Summary

### Problem
Regulatory compliance requires detailed records of all chemical and fertilizer applications including applicator, products, rates, timing, and location.

### Goals
1. Maintain complete application records
2. Track applicator certifications
3. Support regulatory reporting
4. Enable compliance audits
